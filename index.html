Work/Project Experiences

I had the opportunity to work with some of the best engineers on my campus in this fellowhip program. I got the opportunity to dive deep into integrating technology like AI and 3-D printing into health sciences and Nuclear engineering through working with drones and radiation detectors.

Headstarter AI

Over the summer of 2024, I became a software engineer fellow at Headstarter ai, A startup that specializes in integrating AI into business as well as web development projects. I got to work with an amazing group of software engineers on impactful projects and created lifelong experiences and connections.

Personal Financial Tracker

This full stack project is a personal finance tracker which is designed to allow users to sign in and be able to track their finances as well as visualize their bank balance given their paycheck, expenses and how much of their paycheck they save and invest.

Car Price Predictor

This Machine learning project was trained using neural networks and linear regression algorithms to predict the price of a car given its age, miles drive, brand, etc. We added an image detection pipeline with OpenCV to allow users to take pictures of their car and classify damage based on images to increase the versitility of the project.

Smart Cards! AI Study tool

Created a full stack application with Next.js and Firebase that allows users to generate study flashcards and save them in folders. Powered by OpenAI's API.

AI Rate My Professor

Created a full stack application with Next.js and Firebase and Retrieval-augmented generation that allows users to find professors from a large catalog according to specific user input and queries. Powered by OpenAI's API.
